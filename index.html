Welcome!

Hi there! I'm Leticia Leon-Rodriguez. I am Third-Year Computer Science major at Cal Poly SLO. I am always looking to learn new skills and really like to approach problems with an open mind and an outside-the-box thinking strategy.

While at Cal Poly I have been invovled in Cal Poly's Society of Women Engineers and am currently serving as Connections Chair as a part of the Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion Core. I have also had the opportunity to work as an undergraduate research assistant under Dr. Theresa Migler, where I worked on a project that focused on analyzing the social network of Cal Poly's Computing Departments in order to better understand and better foster community and student's mental health.

I have truly enjoyed these experiences and am always looking for more ways to practice Cal Poly's Learn By Doing idology.
